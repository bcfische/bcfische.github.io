Wind Speed



When examining this plot, it is helpful to consider the five major wind zones and where they exist: polar easterlies (poles north and south), westerlies (mid-latitudes), horse latitudes (between 30 and 35 degrees north and south), trade winds (tropics), and the doldrums (near the equator).
